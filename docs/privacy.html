Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Shopify Price Tracker ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Shopify Price Tracker is designed to operate entirely locally on your device. We do not have servers, and we do not transmit your data to any third parties.

2. Local Storage

All data related to tracked products, including URLs, images, names, and price history, is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage mechanism (chrome.storage.local). This data never leaves your computer.

3. Permissions

We request the following permissions to provide the core functionality of the extension:

  • Storage: To save your tracked products and preferences locally.
  • Alarms: To schedule background checks for price updates.
  • Notifications: To alert you when a price drop is detected.
  • Host Permissions (http/https): To allow the extension to access Shopify product pages in the background solely for the purpose of checking prices.

4. Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics tools (like Google Analytics) inside the extension. We do not track your browsing history.
